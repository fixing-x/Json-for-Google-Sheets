Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 14, 2026

1. Introduction

JSON for Sheets ("we", "us", or "our") provides a Google Sheets™ add-on that offers custom functions for processing JSON data. We respect your privacy and represent that we are committed to protecting your information.

2. Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, store, or share your personal data.

The JSON for Sheets add-on runs entirely within your Google Sheets™ environment. All processing of data (parsing, stringifying, filtering) occurs locally in the spreadsheet instance associated with your Google account.

  • We do not have access to your spreadsheet data.
  • We do not transmit your data to any external servers.
  • We do not track your usage of the add-on.

3. Google User Data Policy Compliance

JSON for Sheets' use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4. Data Security

Since all operations are performed within Google's secure infrastructure (Google Apps Script), your data benefits from the same security protections as your Google Sheets files.
